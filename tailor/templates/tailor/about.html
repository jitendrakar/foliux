Crafting Heritage

Precision, Fit & Styling Excellence

TailorX Boutique was founded in New Delhi with a clear objective: to bring high-end custom apparel stitching directly to your doorstep. We believe that clothing should adjust to your body structure, not the other way around.

Every piece we stitch undergoes thorough checks: from fabric cutting alignments to thread margins and final trial checks. We maintain detailed size profiles of every customer, simplifying subsequent order bookings.

Premium Trims

Elite Sizing

Our Vision

To lead the premium custom tailoring market in Delhi by combining digital order tracking, customer profile sizing, and traditional craftsmanship.

Our Mission

To offer a stress-free custom clothing experience through door-to-door sizing, clear trial checking, and consistent quality.

Our Values

We value precise fit integrity, clear pricing structures, eco-friendly fabric suggestions, and respectful customer relationships.
