Our Boutique Story

Experience Premium Sizing & Elite Fashion

Welcome to **TailorX**, where custom clothing meets master craftsmanship. Managed by **Jitendra Kar**, we provide personalized sizing and custom designs from our boutique located in Sriniwaspuri, New Delhi.

Whether it is an executive two-piece suit for work, padded designer blouse styling, or a heavily embroidered bridal lehenga, our master tailors combine traditional handcrafting techniques with modern precision cuts to deliver the perfect fit.
