Meet Dr. Inam Ul Haque

Dr. Inam Ul Haque is a highly regarded Internal Medicine and Diabetes Specialist holding MD and DNB qualifications. With over 15 years of active clinical practice, his clinic provides comprehensive health evaluations and structured recovery pathways for acute and chronic systemic conditions.

We prioritize thorough diagnostics, customized pharmaceutical management, preventive monitoring, and long-term health coaching for lifestyle diseases.

What We Do

Comprehensive Clinical Services

Providing a broad scope of internal medicine assessments and clinical care to secure your medical wellness.

Diabetes Care

Holistic diabetes diagnosis, continuous glucose monitoring programs, personalized diet integration, and advanced therapeutic interventions to prevent diabetic complications.

General Physician

First-contact diagnostic consultation for acute infectious diseases, seasonal viral fevers, respiratory infections, gastrointestinal disorders, and general adult conditions.

Blood Pressure Control

Comprehensive monitoring of primary and secondary hypertension, cardiovascular risk profiling, medication adjustments, and critical target-organ protective therapies.

Thyroid Management

Precise hormonal diagnostic workups, medical management of Hyperthyroidism, Hypothyroidism, thyroid nodules, and structured hormone level balancing protocols.

Preventive Healthcare

Executive health checkups, vaccination schedules for adults, lifestyle health coaching, early cancer screenings, and metabolic disorder prevention guides.

Chronic Disease Management

Long-term therapeutic management pathways for chronic asthma, COPD, arthritis, kidney health diagnostics, and multi-system medical conditions.
